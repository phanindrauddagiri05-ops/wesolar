Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

WeSolar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who use our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information submitted through the WeSolar management platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information through this platform:

  • Customer Information: Name, mobile number, email address, Aadhar number, PAN card number, bank account details, GPS coordinates, and address.
  • Employee/Contractor Information: Name, role, mobile number, Aadhar and PAN card photos provided during account registration.
  • Installation Records: Equipment photos, electrical readings, material usage data, and completion photos.
  • Login Data: Usernames and encrypted passwords used to access the platform.
3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used exclusively for:

  • Managing solar installation projects and tracking progress.
  • Verifying the identity of employees and contractors.
  • Processing loan applications and subsidy claims on behalf of customers.
  • Generating reports and maintaining project records.
  • Communicating important updates to customers and staff.
4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Sensitive fields such as Aadhar numbers and bank account details are masked in the platform and accessible only to authorized admin and office staff.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. Data may only be shared:

  • With authorized government bodies for subsidy processing (e.g., PM Surya Ghar scheme).
  • With financial institutions involved in loan processing, with customer consent.
  • As required by applicable law or court order.
6. Data Retention

Customer and installation data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy and to comply with legal obligations. Employee records are retained during the period of employment or contract and for a reasonable period thereafter.

7. Your Rights

Individuals whose data is collected have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about them.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of their data where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact your WeSolar administrator or reach out to support@wesolar.in.

8. Cookies

This platform uses session cookies necessary for authentication and platform functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the handling of your data, please contact:
